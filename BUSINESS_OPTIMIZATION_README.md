## üî¨ Pattern Discovery

The framework discovers **4 major patterns**:

### Pattern 1: Profit Margin ‚Üí Valuation
Companies with >15% margins valued **significantly higher** than low-margin companies.

**Evidence:** Apple, Microsoft, Alphabet vs Amazon, Walmart

### Pattern 2: Sector Performance
Different sectors have vastly different baseline success metrics.

**Best performers:** Technology (28% avg margins), Finance (22%)
**Challenging:** Retail (4%), Energy (12%)

### Pattern 3: Scale Advantages
Larger companies show **higher profitability** through economies of scale.

**Evidence:** Top 50% by revenue have higher margins than bottom 50%

### Pattern 4: Luxury Premium
Luxury brands command **2-3x higher margins** than mass market.

**Evidence:** LVMH (21.4%) vs Walmart (2.4%)

## üé≤ Outcome Simulation

For each goal, the framework runs **1000 Monte Carlo simulations** considering:
- Base improvement from optimization (15% avg ¬± 5%)
- Pattern-based multipliers (from discovered patterns)
- Lever effectiveness (more levers = more options)
- Market conditions (random shocks)

### Success Probability Categories:
- **>75% = SOLVED WELL** ‚úÖ - High confidence, implement immediately
- **50-75% = PARTIALLY SOLVED** ‚ö° - Moderate confidence, needs refinement
- **<50% = NOT SOLVED** ‚ùå - Low confidence, adjust targets or find new levers

## üìä Interpretation Guide

Goals with **>75% success probability**. These are:
- Backed by strong patterns
- Have multiple effective levers
- Realistic targets
- **Action:** Implement immediately

### What Problems Are Partially Solved?
Goals with **50-75% success probability**. These:
- Show promise but need refinement
- May lack sufficient levers
- Could benefit from additional patterns
- **Action:** Invest in pattern discovery, add levers

### What Problems Are NOT Solved?

- Have overly ambitious targets
- Lack effective levers

## üîß Customization

### Add More Companies
Edit `_ingest_sec_data()` method to add companies:
```python
{'name': 'Your Company', 'cik': 'CIK_NUMBER', 'sector': 'Industry',
 'revenue': 100000000, 'profit_margin': 0.15, 'market_cap': 500000000}
```

### Add Custom Goals
Edit `define_business_optimization_goals()`:
```python
{
    'id': 'G13',
    'name': 'Your Custom Goal',
    'category': 'YourCategory',
    'target_metric': 'your_metric',
    'target_value': 1.0,
    'current_avg': 0.5,
    'priority': 'high',
    'levers': ['lever1', 'lever2']
}
```

### Adjust Simulation Parameters
In `simulate_optimization_outcomes()`:
- Change `n_simulations` (default: 1000)
- Adjust `base_improvement` distribution
- Modify `market_factor` volatility
